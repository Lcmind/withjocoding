Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

FPS Reaction Time Test respects your privacy. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage Data: Your game scores and settings are stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense for advertising. Google may collect cookies and usage data. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game scores are stored locally to track your progress
  • Language preference is saved for better user experience
  • No personal information is collected or transmitted to servers

3. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Storing your language preference
  • Google AdSense advertising (managed by Google)

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense: Ads are served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Clearing your browser data will delete all game records.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through GitHub Issues.
